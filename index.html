Things I love

Figure 1: Plants

Plants are living things that grow on land or in water. From snowy mountain slopes to dry, hot deserts, plants can survive almost anywhere on Earth. Plants can be divided into two groups: flowering plants, for example, sunflowers, orchids, and most types of tree.

Figure 2: Snowboarding

I've been snowboarding for about a year now, and I've been to several slopes. My dream is to get a BURTON snowboard, and learn how to a few flips and spins. There are different levels of difficults at snow resorts; I typically go on the blues (squares/circles).

Figure 3: Cello

I've been playing the cello for almost 6 years now, starting since I was in mid-second grade. I currently play on a 3/4 size, and I'm working on Hadyn Cello Concerto in C. I love playing music, and I enjoy playing in groups, in duets, in competitions/concerts, and in orchestra/chamber.
